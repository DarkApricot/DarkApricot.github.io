Lethal Zombies

This is project Lethal Zombies!


For this project we had to make a tower defense, this was at the height of the Lethal Company popularity and I thought: "Hey, isn't Plants vs. Zombies technically a tower defense?" And thats how we got to this point.
In my version you play as the monsters, defending your home from the Human enemies that want to steal your items.



In this project I made all the 2D art, animations and code within a week.

Original Plants vs. Zombies by PopCap Games.
Original Lethal Company and used art by Zeekerss.
